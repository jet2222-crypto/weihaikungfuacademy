Experiences from international students

Students arrive from different countries and backgrounds, but many describe similar impressions: consistent training, direct teaching, and a focused environment.

Thomas Weber, Germany

I had trained martial arts before, but never in a setting like this. Training every day with direct correction changes how you understand practice completely.

Alejandro Ruiz, Spain

I originally came for a short stay, but the structure and intensity made me want to continue. Every day builds on the last.

Daniel Cooper, United Kingdom

The teaching is serious but not rigid. You are expected to improve, but you are also guided closely so you actually can.

Lucas Ferreira, Brazil

Living in a training environment makes a big difference. You are not just attending classes, you are fully immersed in the process.

Marek Kowalski, Poland

Corrections are immediate and precise. That alone accelerated my progress more than anything I had experienced before.

Ethan Miller, USA

After a longer stay, it started to feel like a second home. The routine and environment create a strong sense of focus and continuity.

Students consistently describe disciplined training, direct correction, and steady long-term progress.
